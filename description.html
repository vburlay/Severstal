Description

Steel is one of the most important building materials of our time. Steel buildings are resistant to natural and artificial wear and which has made this material ubiquitous throughout the world. To improve the efficiency of steel production, this program will help to identify defects.

In this program was used an algorithm by localizing and classifying surface defects on a steel plate.

Dataset Description:
  • Defects must be segmented and classified in the test set.
  • Each image may have no defects, a defect of a single class, or defects of multiple classes.
  • For each image must be segment defects of each class (ClassId = [1, 2, 3, 4]).
